About Us

Kinisi Technologies is a student run start-up out of McMaster University

Kinisi is a posture tracking wearable that provides posture-performance analytics for athletes, specifically rowers at this stage. As Kinisi, we aim to help rowers maintain a proper posture during their training and boost their power using our product that comes with hardware to capture and store the person's posture and software to process and measure their performance improvement.
